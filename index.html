    About

    Tri-Valley Crypto Hacks is the first 24 hour hackathon in the Dublin-Pleasanton-Livermore area for high schoolers. With the help of Girls Who Code and members in the Pleasanton Community, Tri-Valley Crypto Hacks hopes to empower the next generation of bay area youth into learning how the world around them works through the collaborative opportunities that present themselves at its hackathons.

    FAQ

    How do I get help from mentors?

    Mentors will have a form of help rooms from discord voice calls to zoom or skype zoom meetings. Every time a new mentor is available we will alert all attendees there way of communication.

    Is there a fee and what for?

    There is no fee to join this hackathon. This should be a opportunity for you to learn and grow your skills.

    Who can come?

    This hackathon is open to all high schoolers especially from the Tri-Valley Area.

    Will there be any other activities, workshops, or games?

    There will be an intro to HTML/CSS workshop designed for beginner audiences. In the workshop, you will get a brief overview of web design and will design your first basic website.

    Is there a layout of the schedule?

    Yes! If you look on this website under the schedule, you can see a layout of the full 24 hours of the hackathon.

    Why should you come?

    It's quarantine! What else is there to do? Build something that makes the world a better place and tell your friends about it, all without leaving your couch.

    What can I win?

    Cloudsploit credit and amazon gift cards. Prizes may change as the date gets closer
